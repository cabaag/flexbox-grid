Responsive

The system was build to response to different sizes in which includes the columns, offsets, alignment, distribution, show and hide for xs, sm, md and lg sizes. The grid scales up to 12 columns to dispose as device or viewport increase.

<div class='row'>
  <div class='xs-12 sm-4 md-6 lg-8'>
    <div class='box'>Responsive</div>
  </div>
</div>

Fluid

Converts the container into a full width container, just replace .container for .container-fluid 

<div class='container-fluid'>
  <div class='row'>
    <div class='col xs-12 sm-4 md-6 lg-8'>
      <div class='box'>Responsive</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Offsets

Push columns to the rights usign offsets.

<div class='row'>
  <div class='col xs-2 xs-offset-2 md-4 md-offset-4'>
    <div class='box'>Offsets</div>
  </div>
</div>

Custom width

<div class='row'>

    <div class='box'>Offsets</div>
  </div>
